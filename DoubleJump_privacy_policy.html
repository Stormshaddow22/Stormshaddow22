Privacy Policy for Double Jump

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing Double Jump ("we", "our", or "the extension"). We are strictly committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our extension operates and clarifies that we do not collect, store, track, or share any of your personal data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Double Jump does not collect or transmit any personal, sensitive, or user-identifying data. All processing happens entirely locally within your browser context.

To perform its core functions, the extension requests specific technical permissions, utilized strictly as follows:

  • Tabs & Host Permissions (https://phone.foodhub.dev/*): Used solely to discover and connect with your active phone CRM dashboard tab to monitor local availability statuses. No browsing history outside of this specific domain is ever accessed or read.
  • Scripting: Used exclusively to re-inject the status listener script locally if you reload or update the extension while your phone CRM tab is already open.
  • Context Menus: Used only to add a right-click test option to the extension icon so users can manually verify that the local automation engine is operating correctly.

3. Data Transmission and Third-Parties

Because all automation logic runs completely client-side:

  • No data is ever sent over the internet to us or any third-party servers.
  • We do not use any external analytics engines, tracking pixels, or remote database logging.
  • We do not sell, rent, trade, or monetize your information in any way.

4. Data Security

Since no data is collected or stored remotely, there is no risk of your data being compromised via server-side breaches. Your local execution environment is completely protected by standard Google Chrome security boundaries.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect structural changes to the extension. Any updates will be pushed alongside version updates on the Chrome Web Store and will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this privacy policy or the local operation of the extension, please feel free to reach out via our developer contact page on the Chrome Web Store.
