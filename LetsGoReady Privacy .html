Privacy Policy for LetsGoReady

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

LetsGoReady is committed to maintaining the privacy and security of its users. This policy outlines how our extension interacts with your data.

1. Data Collection and Usage

  • No Data Collection: LetsGoReady does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Local Processing: All data processing, including the monitoring of agent counts and status transitions, occurs strictly within the user's local browser environment.
  • No External Transmission: No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties[cite: 1].

2. Permission Justifications

To fulfill its single purpose of automating agent status transitions, LetsGoReady requires the following permissions:

  • storage: Used only to save the extension's ON/OFF toggle state locally[cite: 1, 4].
  • tabs & scripting: Used to programmatically identify work-related tabs and trigger the "Ready" status dropdown[cite: 1, 4].
  • Host Permissions: Access is restricted solely to internal work domains (ms.foodhub.com and phone.foodhub.dev)[cite: 1, 4].

3. Compliance

LetsGoReady complies with the Google Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements[cite: 1].
